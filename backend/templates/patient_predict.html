What do 0, 1, 2, 3 mean?

0: Typical angina, classic heart-related chest pain.

1: Atypical angina, chest pain that is less typical.

2: Non-anginal pain, chest pain not usually linked to angina.

3: Asymptomatic, no chest pain symptoms reported.

What does Resting ECG mean?

This is the resting electrocardiogram result category used by the prediction model.

Maximum heart rate achieved during exercise.

What is Oldpeak?

Oldpeak measures ST depression caused by exercise relative to rest. It helps indicate possible heart stress.

What does ST slope mean?

This is the slope of the peak exercise ST segment on ECG and is encoded as 0, 1, or 2 for the model.
